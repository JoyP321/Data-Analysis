About Police Shooting Data

This site analyzes data from the Washington post's database of police shootings (found here) found on the CORGIS Dataset Project. It is a record of police shootings from 2015 to May of 2019, in whch a police officer, in the line of duty, shot and killed a civilian. Because it was gathered by combing through local news reports, law enforcement websites, and social media as well as by monitoring independent databases, the data may not be 100% complete. The Washington Post continues to update the database as new details and incidents emerge and take place.

In this dataset, "Threat-level" is defined as "attack" (a direct and immediate thret to life to the officer), "other" (consisting of threatening behavior), or "undetermined" (in which the threat level was unknown).

In this dataset, an Age of 0 indicates an unknown age and a name of "TK TK" indicates an unknown name.
